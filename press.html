Kitchen Rush is a privacy-first mobile arcade game by Bonjour Pickleball. It is built for quick reflexes, clean court reads, and short replayable sessions - no account, no ads, no tracking.

The product

Kitchen Rush is a browser-based pickleball-inspired game with a mobile-first control scheme. It focuses on reflexes, positioning, timing, and the pressure of the Kitchen rather than full match simulation.

Classic is the core mode. Rush is a faster secondary variant. Daily Challenge is a daily version of Classic.
