Privacy Policy

Effective date: 23 February 2026

1. Who we are

Kitchen Rush is a game published by its creator.
Contact for data and privacy questions: Contact us

Related: Terms of Service

2. Data stored on your device

Kitchen Rush stores game data only in your browser storage to make the game work. This includes game progress, scores, run history, and feature states.

This data:

  • never leaves your device automatically
  • is not sent to our servers
  • can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser storage

Legal basis: performance of the service (Article 6.1.b GDPR).

3. Data we do not collect

We do not collect:

  • names, emails, or any personally identifiable information
  • usage analytics or tracking data
  • cookies (other than browser storage for gameplay)

There are no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts on Kitchen Rush.

4. Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your payment details. Stripe processes your data under its own Privacy Policy.

The only payment-related data stored locally is a premium activation code generated in your browser. This code never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

5. Voluntary data sharing

Kitchen Rush may offer optional features that let you share anonymous gameplay statistics via email. This is always explicit, opt-in, and fully transparent: you see the exact data before sending.

No data is sent automatically. No data is collected without your action.

6. Email communications

If you contact us voluntarily (via email or support link), we may use your email address solely to respond. We do not add you to any mailing list without explicit consent.

7. Children

Kitchen Rush does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has shared personal information, contact us.

8. Your rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access: Your game data is visible in your browser's developer tools
  • Deletion: Clear your browser storage to delete all game data
  • Portability: Game data is standard JSON in localStorage

For any privacy-related request, contact: Contact us

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
